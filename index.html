About Me

Hi! I’m Amirali Vahidi, a curious and adventurous soul who, besides having a deep love for nature, also has a passion for connecting with people and experiencing the richness of life. I believe in learning from everyone I meet and enjoying every moment of communication.

📚 My Academic Life

I’m currently in my final year studying computer engineering at the Department of ECE at the University of Tehran, maintaining a GPA of 3.71/4.
My main academic focus is on security and blockchain technology. I’ve immersed myself in these fields through self-learning and had the privilege of teaching a cryptography and blockchain course during the ACM Summer of Code program at the University of Tehran.

I also had the honor of serving as the chair elected official of the ACM student branch for the 2023-2024 academic year.
